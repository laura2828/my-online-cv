Education and Trainings

Being born and raised in a small town in Maramures, I went to highschool into a field where my analytical thinking could be sharpened: in the Mathematics and Computer Science Class

After that, Chemistry and I had... chemistry... so I went to the Chemical Engineering Faculty

To be more accurate, I learned about: "Engineering and Information Technology of Chemical and Biochemical Processes" - sorry if you were bored while reading this long title :)

Besides the cool lab experiments and interesting chemical structures, I learned MATLAB and ChemCAD - used for Chemical Processes Simulations, learned about Artifficial Inteligence and Automation of Chemical Processes


I have worked in a big company for two years after finishing college, and I worked with a remote team from Norway and with factories across Europe in order to provide technical solutions to customers. Here I learned a lot about different cultures and collaborated very well with both local and remote team and managers. I also loved the problem solving part and offering solutions to customers.


Being familiar with programming and having the desire to change the field I worked in, in 2017 I decided to pursue the FRONT-END WEB DEVELOPMENT Course at The Informal School of IT.

I have accumulated a lot of knowledge about:

  • JavaScript - Functions, Scope, Closure, OOP understanding
  • jQuery
  • ECMAScript 6 - Scoping, Arrow functions
  • AJAX
  • ReactJS - basic knowledge

Therefore, I am a fresh graduate of this course and I love to play around with code in order to create cool stuff! I have gained a little experience while working on the Final project of the course and I really enjoyed working with the team in order to create the application found on this Git repository.
